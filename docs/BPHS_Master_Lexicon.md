# BPHS Master Lexicon

A manually curated, comprehensive lexicon of technical Sanskrit and astrological terms derived from the Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra (BPHS). This document serves as the "Source of Truth" for shloka-level tagging and advanced analysis.

## 1. Graha (Planets & Cosmic Entities)
*Primary celestial bodies and shadow planets.*

- **Surya** (Sun, Ravi, Arka, Divanatha)
- **Chandra** (Moon, Soma, Kumudabandhava)
- **Mangala** (Mars, Kuja, Bhuputra)
- **Budha** (Mercury, Dharatmaja)
- **Guru** (Jupiter, Devejya, Jiva)
- **Shukra** (Venus, Bhargava, Kavi)
- **Shani** (Saturn, Sauri, Chhayasunu)
- **Rahu** (North Node, Svarbhanu)
- **Ketu** (South Node, Shikhi)
- **Upagrahas**: Gulika, Mandi, Dhuma, Vyatipata, Paridhi, Indrachapa, Upaketu.

## 2. Rashi (Zodiac Signs)
*The twelve signs of the sidereal zodiac.*

- **Mesha** (Aries)
- **Vrishabha** (Taurus)
- **Mithuna** (Gemini)
- **Karka** (Cancer)
- **Simha** (Leo)
- **Kanya** (Virgo)
- **Tula** (Libra)
- **Vrishchika** (Scorpio)
- **Dhanu** (Sagittarius)
- **Makara** (Capricorn)
- **Kumbha** (Aquarius)
- **Meena** (Pisces)

## 3. Bhava (Houses & Lagnas)
*Divisions of space and calculation points.*

- **Lagna** (Ascendant, Tanu Bhava)
- **Dhana Bhava** (2nd House)
- **Sahaja Bhava** (3rd House, Vikrama)
- **Sukha Bhava** (4th House)
- **Putra Bhava** (5th House)
- **Ari Bhava** (6th House, Shashta)
- **Yuvati Bhava** (7th House, Jaya)
- **Randhra Bhava** (8th House, Ayur)
- **Dharma Bhava** (9th House, Bhagya)
- **Karma Bhava** (10th House, Vyoma)
- **Labha Bhava** (11th House)
- **Vyaya Bhava** (12th House)
- **Special Lagnas**: Hora Lagna, Ghati Lagna, Bhava Lagna, Varnada Lagna, Pranapada Lagna.
- **Arudha Padas**: Arudha Lagna (Pada), Darapada, Bhagyapada, etc.

## 4. Nakshatra (Lunar Mansions)
*The 27 constellations used for detailed timing and attributes.*

- **Ashwini**, **Bharani**, **Krittika**, **Rohini**, **Mrigashira**, **Ardra**, **Punarvasu**, **Pushya**, **Ashlesha**.
- **Magha**, **Purva Phalguni**, **Uttara Phalguni**, **Hasta**, **Chitra**, **Swati**, **Vishakha**, **Anuradha**, **Jyeshtha**.
- **Mula**, **Purvashadha**, **Uttarashadha**, **Shravana**, **Dhanishta**, **Shatabhisha**, **Purva Bhadrapada**, **Uttara Bhadrapada**, **Revati**.
- **Abhijit** (The 28th intercalary Nakshatra).

## 5. Varga (Divisional Charts)
*The Shodashavarga (16 divisions) for granular analysis.*

- **Rashi** (D1)
- **Hora** (D2)
- **Drekkana** (D3)
- **Chaturthansha** (D4, Turyansha)
- **Saptamansha** (D7)
- **Navamsha** (D9)
- **Dashamansha** (D10)
- **Dwadashansha** (D12)
- **Shodashansha** (D16)
- **Vimshansha** (D20)
- **Chaturvimshansha** (D24, Siddhansha)
- **Saptavimshansha** (D27, Bhansha)
- **Trimshansha** (D30)
- **Khavedansha** (D40)
- **Akshavedansha** (D45)
- **Shashtyansha** (D60)

## 6. Bala (Planetary & House Strength)
*Calculation systems for determining power.*

- **Shadbala**: Sthana Bala, Dig Bala, Kala Bala, Chesta Bala, Naisargika Bala, Drig Bala.
- **Vimshopaka**: Point system for divisional strength (20-point scale).
- **Ashtakavarga**: Bindu (points), Rekha (lines), Samudaya Ashtakavarga.
- **Ishta/Kashta**: Auspicious and inauspicious results of planets.

## 7. Dasha (Timing Systems)
*Time periods for the fructification of results.*

- **Vimshottari Dasha** (120-year cycle)
- **Kalachakra Dasha** (Based on Moon's Navamsha)
- **Chara Dasha** (Jaimini system used in BPHS)
- **Varnada Dasha**
- **Yogini Dasha**
- **Antardasha**, **Pratyantardasha**, **Sookshmadasha**, **Pranadasha**.

## 8. Yoga (Combinations & Formations)
*Specific planetary arrangements and their results.*

- **Nabhasa Yogas**: Akriti, Sankhya, Ashraya, Dala Yogas.
- **Pancha Mahapurusha Yogas**: Ruchaka, Bhadra, Hamsa, Malavya, Shasha.
- **Rajayogas**: Combinations of Kendra and Trikona lords.
- **Dhana Yogas**: Wealth combinations.
- **Daridra Yogas**: Poverty combinations.
- **Adhi Yoga**, **Amala Yoga**, **Parvata Yoga**, **Kahala Yoga**.

## 9. Calculation & Technical Concepts
*Mathematical principles and terminology.*

- **Spashta**: Precise longitude (Graha Spashta, Bhava Spashta).
- **Trikona Shodhana**: Trine reduction in Ashtakavarga.
- **Ekadhipatya Shodhana**: Reduction for signs with same lord.
- **Ayurdaya**: Calculation of longevity (Pindayu, Amshayu, Nisargayu).
- **Drishti**: Planetary aspect (Graha Drishti, Rashi Drishti).
- **Argala**: Intervention/obstruction from specific houses.

## 10. General Astrological Concepts
*Thematic groupings.*

- **Arishta**: Misfortune or death-inflicting combinations (Balarishta, Yogarishta).
- **Maraka**: Killer planets/houses.
- **Avastha**: Planetary states (Shayanadi Avasthas, Baladi Avasthas).
- **Karaka**: Significators (Atmakaraka, Amatyakaraka... Sthira Karakas).
- **Punya/Papa**: Benefic and Malefic influences.
- **Moksha/Dharma/Artha/Kama**: The four aims of life.
