# Samudrika Tilaka — सामुद्रिकतिलक

Also known as: *Nara-lakshana-shastra*

**Author:** Durlabhara; completed by his son Jagaddeva
**Period:** 12th century CE (c. 1160–1175)
**Verses:** 300+
**Status: Not yet digitized in this collection**

Structured as a dialogue. Covers head-to-toe body marks and their interpretation for predicting a person's destiny. A manuscript is held in the British Museum. One of the few Samudrika texts with a known author and approximate date.

Available at: British Museum manuscript collection; partial digitization through academic sources.
