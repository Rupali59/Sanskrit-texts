# Vedanga Jyotisha — वेदाङ्ग ज्योतिषम्

The oldest stratum of Jyotish literature. The Vedanga Jyotisha is one of the six Vedangas (auxiliary disciplines of the Vedas), concerned with the proper timing of Vedic rituals through astronomical calculation.

Attributed to the sage **Lagadha**. Two recensions survive; the Samaveda and Atharvaveda versions are lost.

The Yajurveda recension (43 verses) is slightly longer than the Rigveda recension (36 verses) — 29 verses are shared between them. Dating is uncertain; scholarly estimates range from 700 BCE to 4th century BCE.
