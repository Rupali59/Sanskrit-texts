# Siddhanta — सिद्धान्त (Mathematical Astronomy)

The Siddhanta skandha is the mathematical and computational foundation of Jyotish — planetary positions, orbital mechanics, eclipse prediction, trigonometry, and calendar systems. These texts established the astronomical framework within which Hora and Samhita operate.

The word *Siddhanta* means "established conclusion" or "demonstrated truth."

## Notes on the Panchasiddhantika

Varahamihira's *Panchasiddhantika* is a compilation and comparison of five earlier astronomical systems (Surya, Romaka, Paulisa, Vasishtha, Paitamaha Siddhantas). It bridges the Siddhanta and Hora traditions and is a critical source for understanding the state of Indian astronomy in the 6th century.
