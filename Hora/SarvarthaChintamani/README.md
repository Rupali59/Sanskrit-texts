# Sarvartha Chintamani — सर्वार्थचिन्तामणि

**Author:** Venkatesa Sharma
**Period:** 13th century CE
**Status: Not yet digitized in this collection**

A comprehensive Hora text notable for its depth on house significations — goes further than predecessors in detailing the effects of planets in each house and sign. Widely used in South Indian Jyotish practice.

Available at: Internet Archive, various Sanskrit publishers.
