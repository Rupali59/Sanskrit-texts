# Hora — होरा (Natal Astrology)

The Hora skandha covers the interpretation of birth charts (janma kundali) — planetary positions at the moment of birth, their effects on the native's life, health, relationships, career, longevity, and death.

The term *Hora* derives from *Aho-Ratra* (day-night), compressed to its middle syllable. It is the most voluminous branch of classical Jyotish.
