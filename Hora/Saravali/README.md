# Saravali — सारावली

**Author:** Kalyanavarma
**Period:** 8th–9th century CE
**Verses:** ~4,000 (55 chapters)
**Status: Not yet digitized in this collection**

One of the five foundational texts of classical Hora. Kalyanavarma was a king who compiled techniques from Parashara, Garga, and Varahamihira. The Saravali is especially valued for its treatment of yogas (planetary combinations), rising signs, and dasha results.

Covers: planetary natures, sign placements, house effects, yogas, longevity calculation, special lagnas.

Available at: https://archive.org/details/saravaliofkalyanavarmasanthanamr.astrology_202003_28_Z
