# Uttara Kalamrita — उत्तरकालामृत

**Author:** Kalidasa (attributed; distinct from the poet)
**Period:** Medieval (date uncertain)
**Status: Not yet digitized in this collection**

A significant Hora text in two parts: Kalamrita and Uttara Kalamrita. Known for its treatment of the Karakas (significators) and special yogas. Widely referenced in North Indian Jyotish alongside Brihat Parashara.

Available at: Internet Archive, Sagar Publications.
